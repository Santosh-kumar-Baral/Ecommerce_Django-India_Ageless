About Us

Welcome to our website, dedicated to providing comprehensive information about Geographical Indication (GI) tags in India. Our team is passionate about promoting and preserving India's unique cultural and agricultural heritage. We believe that GI tags are crucial for protecting and promoting India's diverse traditional products and practices, and we aim to increase awareness and understanding of their importance.

Our website features detailed information about the history, origin, and benefits behind the GI tag system in India. We also provide a directory of all the GI-tagged products and their respective regions, allowing visitors to explore and discover the amazing diversity of India's local products. Whether you're a consumer, tourist, producer, or policymaker, our website is the go-to resource for accurate and up-to-date information about GI tags in India. Thank you for visiting, and please don't hesitate to contact us with any questions or feedback.
